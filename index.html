Who's behind BuiltByMK

A coach who builds
for coaches

I'm MK — a fitness coach based in Doha, Qatar, working with the over-40 crowd. People who are too busy, too sore, or too stuck in the wrong routine to figure out where to start. Through Fitbodies by MK, I coach clients through strength training, body recomposition, and building consistency that actually holds after 40.

After building my own coaching programs, client tools, and websites from scratch, I realised most coaches were either overpaying agencies or making do with something that didn't represent them. So I started BuiltByMK — to help other coaches get online properly, fast, without the nonsense.

Every build comes from actually knowing what coaches need and what their clients respond to. You work directly with me — not a team, not a template, not an account manager. Just straight, focused work from first message to going live.
